Join Us



BUILD is a fantastic way to apply your knowledge to impact lives. You would be working in small teams to build technological solutions for nonprofits and local businesses. Our work allows organizations to spend less time on maintaining and developing their technical infrastructure and more time focusing on their missions to help and serve others.


BUILD teams consist of one Project Manager and approximately three Project Developers. The Project Manager leads the team and initially meets with our clients to get an understanding of their problem and their requirements. Then, with the help of the Project Developers, the team develops a plan and timeline for the project. Teams will meet on a weekly basis to collaborate on ideas and ensure the project remains on track.
